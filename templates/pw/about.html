ParkWhale & the 1000 Whaling Corpses: About Us

Ahoy there, matey. Our crew of four welcome you with loads of heart-rending wailing.

ParkWhale is here to satisfy all your Vancouver-based street parking needs. Use this site to check in advance for the parking rates and times so you can avoid paying loads when all those free parking spots are taken. And figure out which street nearby might just have slightly cheaper parking! Moreover, check out parking spots dedicated for those with disabilities or motorcycles. We got 'em all, no fear.

So you're still reading, eh?

Well then, matey, lemme tell you all about our team.

We be a team of four mighty undergrads at UBC (as of 2015) studying computer science. This project was created as a project for one of our CS courses. The goal of the project was to make a webapp within the 4 months of the course, following the Scrum framework and based on various criteria. One of these requirements was to make use of datasets provided by the City of Vancouver.
